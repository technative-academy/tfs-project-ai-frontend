About Sandwich Time

Who we are and the people between the bread

Based by the sea in Sandwich Bay in Kent, our seaview café and offices are the perfect spot to relax with a sandwich.

Founded in 2000, we have become the most popular sandwich shop in the South-East and cater for over 100 events per year.
